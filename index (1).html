How Eco-bricks are formed

Eco Bricks

Eco-bricks are a revolutionary product developed by Technokrates, a company dedicated to finding innovative and eco-friendly solutions. These plastic bricks are designed to be used in pavers and offer a number of benefits over traditional concrete bricks. Not only do eco-bricks reduce plastic waste and the pollution caused by it, but they are also stronger and more durable than concrete bricks. Additionally, eco-bricks are a more sustainable and environmentally friendly option for pavers, as they are made from recycled plastic rather than using new resources. By choosing eco-bricks for your paver projects, you can not only contribute to a cleaner environment, but also enjoy the benefits of a stronger and more durable paver surface
